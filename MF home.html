About Us

Hadiyyah R., Amariah M., and Nessa E. came together discussing the common problem that people go through everyday, stress. Stress is based of many things, and an easy way to take it out is on food. Our goal is to reduce your stress through food in the healthiest way possible, so eat up!

FAQs

Why did you create this website? We all Related on the fact that stress eating is a real issue, especially with unhealthy foods. We wanted to provide an alternative to certain foods to make your stress reduced, as well as get you to eat healthy.

How did you meet? We met during a 7-week Summer immersion program in Atlanta, Georgia. A summer immersion program for girls to learn the basics of coding called "Girls Who Code"
